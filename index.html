Brief Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at the Computer Science Department, UNC-Chapel Hill. I am working with Professor Marc Niethammer.

I obtained my Master's degree in College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, working with Professor Dong C. Liu. I graduated with a B.E. in Computer Science from the same University in 2007. I was a visiting student at Shenzhen Insititute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and advised by Professor Yangsheng Xu.

My research interests are including Machine Learning, Image Analysis and Computer Vision. Resume
